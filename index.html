the GEMS (generative expression of modeled shapes) project has been a fun summer project for me. all you need to do is enter any 10 letters and/ or numbers to generate a 3D shape or "gem" with its own custom shader.

In a brief explanation, the input value or the seed gets many of its properties examined. The most common number within the seed, the divisibility of the seed under certain values, its digit sum, and other properties all get measured to see what properties they will have on the shape like how many colors the gem will have or how the textures will be blended together.
